*----------------------------------------------------------------------*
* INITIALIZATION
*----------------------------------------------------------------------*
INITIALIZATION.
  PERFORM FRM_INIT_PROG.

*----------------------------------------------------------------------*
* AT SELECTION-SCREEN ON VALUE-REQUEST
*----------------------------------------------------------------------*
AT SELECTION-SCREEN ON VALUE-REQUEST FOR P_HKONT.
  PERFORM FRM_F4HELP_HKONT.

AT SELECTION-SCREEN ON VALUE-REQUEST FOR P_SPMON.
  PERFORM FRM_F4HELP_JZMON.

*----------------------------------------------------------------------*
* AT SELECTION-SCREEN OUTPUT
*----------------------------------------------------------------------*
AT SELECTION-SCREEN OUTPUT.
  PERFORM FRM_OUTPUT_SCREEN.

*----------------------------------------------------------------------*
* AT SELECTION-SCREEN
*----------------------------------------------------------------------*
AT SELECTION-SCREEN.
  PERFORM FRM_CHECK_SCREEN.

*&---------------------------------------------------------------------*
* START-OF-SELECTION
*&---------------------------------------------------------------------*
START-OF-SELECTION.
  CASE ABAP_TRUE.
    WHEN RD_WHDZ. "录入银行对账单
      PERFORM FRM_WHDZ_DZD.
    WHEN RD_ZDDZ. "银行票据自动对账
      PERFORM FRM_ZDDZ_DZD.
      PERFORM FRM_DISP_DZD.
    WHEN RD_SGDZ. "银行票据手工对账
      CALL SCREEN 9000.
    WHEN RD_QXDZ. "取消银行对账
      PERFORM FRM_DISP_QXD.
    WHEN RD_CXDZ. "查询银行对账单
      PERFORM FRM_DISP_CXD.
    WHEN RD_DYDZ. "打印银行余额调节表
      PERFORM FRM_PRNT_DZD.
    WHEN RD_WDQY. "期初企业未达账维护
      PERFORM FRM_WDQY_QYZ.
    WHEN RD_WDYH. "期初银行对账单维护
      PERFORM FRM_WDYH_QYZ.
    WHEN OTHERS.
  ENDCASE.
